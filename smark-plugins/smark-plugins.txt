Smark plugins
#############

A colection of plugins for the Smark documentation tool

Usage:

. export SMARK_PATH=$TOOLTREE_DIR/smark-plugins/?.lua
. smark -o mydoc.html mydoc.txt


Example:

. .luacode
.   local function hello()
.     print 'Hello, world!'
.   end

.luacode
  local function hello()
    print 'Hello, world!'
  end
